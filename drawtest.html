No Tipping Game



The No Tipping Game is a game where you place weights on a board. In this two player game, players take turn placing each of their weights onto the board in phase 1 and remove the weights on the board in phase 2. If after any play, the placement of a block causes the board to tip, then the player who did that play loses. In phase 2, player 2 is limited to remove its own weights until there is none of player 2's weights on the board. This game is configurable so you can set the inital weight of the board, how long you want the board to be, and how many weights each player can have. GL HF.
